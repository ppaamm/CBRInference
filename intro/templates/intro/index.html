AI-assisted language learning
Demonstration for the group on Human-Centric Machine Learning

This application is a demonstration of a research project developed in the research group on Human-centric machine learning. It is thought as an extension of the article Inferring Case-Based Reasoners’ Knowledge to Enhance Interactivity (Murena & Al-Ghossein, 2021). It is still work in progress, so several aspects of this demonstration will be fixed and made better in the future.

This application is developped for demonstration purposes only. No data will be collected during the demonstration.

The application has been developed with the help of Lucas Martens and Moritz Haußner.
