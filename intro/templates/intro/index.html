AI-assisted vocabulary learning
Demonstration for the group on Human-Centric Machine Learning

This application is a demonstration of a research project developed in the research group on Human-centric machine learning. It is thought as an extension of the article Inferring Case-Based Reasoners’ Knowledge to Enhance Interactivity (Murena & Al-Ghossein, 2021).

This application is developped for demonstration purposes only. No data will be collected during the demonstration.

The application has been developed with the help of Lucas Martens and Moritz Haußner.
