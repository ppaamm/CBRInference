Rules

General goal: The general goal of this demonstration is to show how an advanced AI teacher can help you learn complex grammar rules. In this demonstration, we chose the inessive case in Finnish, which is used to express the notion of being inside something.

In order to see how well you master the inessive case, you will be asked {{ N_WORDS_TEST }} questions of the form "What is the inessive of the following word?".

The learning will be organized in 4 short steps:

  • Independent learning: You will pick {{ N_CARDS }} cards from the deck. Each card contains either an example of inessive, or the explanations for a simple rule.
  • Initial test: You pass the test a first time. To answer, use the information contained in your cards! Once the test is done, you will get your score, but no feedback on your mistakes.
  • Guided learning: From your answers, the AI teacher will guess the cards you picked, but also suggest a few additional cards for you to get a better understanding of the inessive case.
  • Final test: You pass the test a second time. Once the test is done, you will get your score. Can the AI teacher understand what you know and help you become better?
